The Beginning

A typical pandemic starts with an infected individual.

Symptoms Emerge

They start having symptoms and become infectious.

Spread Begins

Someone else gets infected.

Further Spread

Then a few more.

Symptoms Spread

Those people start having symptoms and become infectious.

Rapid Growth

More people get infected.

Detection Point

There is now a cluster of unusual cases. Perhaps at this point doctors notice something unusual is happening and raise the alarm.
